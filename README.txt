## 🌟 About The Project

**Couple Game** is a standalone, browser-based party game that brings the classic **Truth or Dare** experience to your device. Designed with a mobile-first approach, it is perfect for couples or close friends to play by simply passing the phone back and forth. 

No server, no internet connection, and no app installation required—just open the HTML file and start playing!

### ✨ Features

- **Classic Truth or Dare**: Enjoy the timeless party game with a modern digital twist.
- **Role-Based Gameplay**: Select between "Girl" and "Boy" profiles to get tailored prompts.
- **Custom Question Bank**: Fully personalize your game! Add your own prompts for:
  - 💬 Girl Truth Questions
  - 🎯 Girl Dares
  - 💬 Boy Truth Questions
  - 🎯 Boy Dares
- **Interactive Dice Roll**: Click the virtual dice (🎲) to add randomness and excitement to every turn.
- **Offline & Portable**: Runs entirely in the browser as a single HTML file.
- **Party Games Hub**: Serves as a central menu for other mini-games (e.g., *Two-player Ludo*).

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Choose Roles**: Set up the players by selecting "Girl" and "Boy".
2. **Roll the Dice**: Click the dice icon (🎲) on the main screen to start the round.
3. **Pick Your Fate**: Choose either **Truth** or **Dare**.
4. **Reveal & Act**: Read the randomly selected prompt. Answer honestly or complete the dare!
5. **Confirm & Pass**: Click **Confirm** once the turn is completed, then hit **Play Again** to pass the device to the next player.

---

## 🛠️ Customizing Your Game

Want to make it more personal? 
1. Navigate to the **Custom Question Bank** from the main menu.
2. Enter your own questions and dares in the text areas (Enter **one question per line**).
3. Click **Save** to apply your custom prompts to the game pool. *(Note: Custom questions are saved in your browser's Local Storage).*
